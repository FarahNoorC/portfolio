Who I am

Data meets
human impact.

I'm Farah Noor Chowdhury — a data enthusiast, problem solver, and someone deeply committed to using technology to make a real difference in people's lives.

With a background in Computer Science and a strong foundation in data science, my career path has led me through a unique blend of humanitarian work, technical roles, and community engagement. Whether it's analyzing datasets, leading field teams, or mentoring future coders, I've always aimed to be at the intersection of tech and social impact.

Most recently, I worked as a WASH Monitoring & Reporting Officer with Action contre la Faim (ACF) in Cox's Bazar, developing monitoring tools and coordinating with local communities to ensure interventions were effective and informed by real feedback.

I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering and recently completed the IBM Data Science Professional Certificate. I'm fluent in English and Bengali, and steadily improving my Italian since moving to Rome.
